Sustainable Travel

Sustainable travel is a way to travel that minimizes environmental impact and supports local communities.
Here are some tips for sustainable travel in Dana Biosphere Reserve:


  • Choose eco-friendly transportation options, such as public transportation, walking, or biking.

  • Stay in locally-owned and operated accommodations.

  • Eat at local restaurants that serve fresh, seasonal food.

  • Purchase souvenirs from local artisans and businesses.

  • Be respectful of the environment and avoid littering.
